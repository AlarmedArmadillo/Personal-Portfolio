# Business System & Funnel

## The Core Model
Teaching email marketing as a reliable monetisation method — proving it by doing it myself first, then selling the systems and services.

## The Funnel (Traffic → Revenue, Repeat)
```
TRAFFIC
  ↓ (Threads, content, personal brand)
LEAD CAPTURE
  ↓ (newsletter opt-in, lead magnet, Beehiiv/Stacked)
NURTURE
  ↓ (email sequences, value delivery, building trust)
SELL
  ↓ (services, products, systems — email marketing related)
REPEAT
  ↓ (retain, upsell, referral)
```

## Services Direction
Monetisation through online communication — specifically:
- Email marketing setup and strategy
- Sales copywriting and sequences
- Systems for predictable lead generation and conversion

## The Stacked Newsletter
The Mango Media newsletter. Core nurture and distribution asset. Lives under the Mango Media entity but feeds the personal brand audience.

## Proof Strategy
Don't sell what you haven't done. Document the journey of building the funnel yourself — the results become the case study that sells the service.

## Key Metrics to Track (when relevant)
- Threads: views, follows, replies per post
- Newsletter: open rate, click rate, growth rate
- Funnel: leads captured, conversion rate at each stage
